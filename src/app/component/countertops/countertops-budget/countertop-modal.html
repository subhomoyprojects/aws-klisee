Assumptions for square footage of flooring

  • Square feet of counters (a+b+c+d+...) * 26 (width of counters) / 144{{counter}}
  • Square feet of island (length 1 * width 1 + length 2 * width 2 + length 3 * width 3) / 144{{island}}
  • Square feet of backsplash (linear inches / 36 * cost Pr/Sf){{backsplash}}
  • Total Square footage (Square feet of counters + Square feet of backsplash){{footage}}
